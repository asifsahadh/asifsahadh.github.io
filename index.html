Publications

Efficient Parameter Estimation by Fine Tuning DistilBERT for Synthetic Text Detection

IEEE | Feb 23, 2026

This study presents a lightweight text classification model designed to distinguish human-written content from text generated by large language models. Using transfer learning on a large and diverse dataset of 1.1 million samples from over 62 LLMs, the approach applies layer selective fine tuning to DistilBERT. This reduces computational cost while maintaining strong generalization and avoiding overfitting. The resulting model achieves high performance, including 97.10% accuracy, strong F1 score, excellent AUROC, and a low false negative rate.
